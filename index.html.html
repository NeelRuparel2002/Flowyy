Product Features
01
Next Gen Filtration Tech

A water filtration bottle is a portable water treatment solution that allows users to purify water while on the go. These bottles typically use a combination of physical and chemical filtration methods to remove impurities and contaminants from the water, such as bacteria, viruses, heavy metals, and chlorine.

02
Water Consumption Tracking

Our model is equipped with tracking system which tracks, how much amount of water has been consumed and how much water is to be consumed by the user.

03
Nutrient Addtion

By using the feature, the user can make the water rich in desired nutrients.

About Company

As 70% of our body is comprised of water and many of important minerals are obtained from the water we drink, it is important that the water we consume is pure and rich in nutrients.

So we have designed a bottle which will be lifeline for frequent travellers and for the people who are unable to get pure and nutrient rich water in their localities.
Various smart features of the bottle include measuring and tracking water consumed by the user in a day which will be helpful to professional athletes, heating technology which will be useful in day to day life and nutrients adding feature which will help people with a particular vitamin or mineral deficiency.
